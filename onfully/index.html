About Us

We’ve skilled in wide range of web and
Other digital market tools.

At the time when Corona is spreading all over the world, when the very future of the people is in dire straits, we are introducing a service-oriented social security plan that serves as a service-oriented application that can benefit everyone including retailers, small entrepreneurs, ordinary workers in various fields and unemployed people in our country. Over last 2 to 3 years Onfully marketing services like Onshoppy application and other related services are brought to people through the studies and efforts in the field of marketing. Onshoppy is not just an online delivery application, Through onshoppy system, benefits of home delivery system, which is now available only to large retailers and foreign companies, can be utilized by all retailers and small entrepreneurs in all sectors of our country, including cash on delivery system. The services of onshoppy to retailers are absolutely free of charge. Customes can get delivery from onshoppy retailers through a system set up by company. Onshoppy provides innovative practices to promote local farmers such as ORGANIC FARMING, HYDROPONICS, AQUAPONICS to promote agriculture, and to market the agro-non-agricultural products they produce across Kerala To provide the offer rate to the customers through small scale retailers which are only available in big super market now a days.This will enable us to deliver the goods through Daily Offers to the shops in our country and thereby attracting more customers to them and increase sales.  'Onshoppy' is a system for promoting local shops and local products.   Onshoppy marketing of local products produced by small entrepreneurs, women's groups, Kudumbasree units etc. across Kerala with the concept of 'One Home One Enterprise' provides an opportunity for their products to be branded. Start up Co-operative Production Units to alleviate the unemployment problem of the youth in our country and to promote the products of our country. To take an initiative to plan and implement ideas that will help the people in the areas of health and education in Kerala as the second phase of the project. The company finds local co-ordinators in all the panchayats in Kerala to coordinate with the onshoppy application and related services on panchayat basis. The Panchayat Coordinator will be responsible for coordinating and controlling the socio-economic affairs of the respective panchayats on behalf of the company. Onfully Marketing Limited provides training and technical assistance to the appointed coordinators so that they can ensure a good career and income.     Initially, each panchayat will be provided with technical assistance to set up various online services of ONSERVICES for the public on the model of Janasevana Kendra / Akshaya Kendra, thereby enabling the coordination and implementation of activities in the respective panchayats.
